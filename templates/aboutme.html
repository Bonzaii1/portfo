About Me!

Hi there! I'm a passionate and driven individual currently pursuing my engineering degree at the University of Montemorelos. As a student, I've not only delved into the world of systems engineering and data science but have also developed a keen interest in these fields. The art of understanding and optimizing complex systems has truly captured my imagination, and I'm enthusiastic about the possibilities it offers.

One of the most rewarding experiences in my journey so far has been my involvement in the Student Management System at my university. I've had the opportunity to contribute to and enhance this system, and what's even more exciting is the chance to take this valuable experience and apply it to universities in the Pacific. It's a unique blend of problem-solving and innovation, and I find it incredibly satisfying to make systems run efficiently and seamlessly for the benefit of students and staff.

Outside the world of academia and engineering, I'm a huge fan of sports and video games. These have been more than just pastimes for me; they've been a way to connect, socialize, and build lasting friendships. Whether it's playing a round of football or teaming up in the latest video game, these experiences have given me a sense of belonging and camaraderie. I'm eager to find opportunities to be part of organizations related to these passions, as I believe in giving back to the sources of happiness and memories in my life.

In a nutshell, I'm a budding engineer with a deep love for systems, a fascination with data, and a heart for collaboration. I'm excited about the journey ahead and the chance to contribute to organizations that align with my interests and values. Let's connect and explore the possibilities together!
